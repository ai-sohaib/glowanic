# Glowanic Launch Keyword Strategy

The initial architecture was created from the uploaded beauty keyword workbook.

## Primary departments

1. **Skin Care** — broad demand around cleansers, moisturizers, sunscreen, serums, face masks, eye care, lip care and body skin care.
2. **Hair Care** — strong demand around shampoo, oils, masks, styling products, color care and scalp treatments.
3. **Beauty Tools** — high-interest shopping categories such as brushes, eyelash curlers, dryers, flat irons, clippers, nail tools and facial devices.

## Lower-difficulty launch opportunities used in the repository

- acne-focused serum and cream topics
- gel moisturizer
- charcoal face mask
- hair spray
- deep conditioning treatment
- thickening powder
- flat iron
- facial steamer
- ice roller
- manicure and pedicure tools
- body scrubber

## Content clusters

- `/category/skin-care/` supported by acne, serum and glow-cream guides
- `/category/hair-care/` supported by treatment, styling and heat-protection guides
- `/category/beauty-tools/` supported by flat-iron, facial-steamer and at-home tool guides

Do not create dozens of thin pages for every keyword. Expand each cluster with useful comparison, routine and safety content first.
