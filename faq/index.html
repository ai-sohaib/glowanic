Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about Glowanic recommendations, Amazon links, prices, orders and the Glowanic bag.

Does Glowanic sell products directly?

No. Glowanic provides editorial recommendations and sends shoppers to Amazon to complete purchases.

Why are prices not displayed?

Amazon prices and availability change frequently. Confirm current information on the Amazon listing.

What is the Glowanic bag?

It is a browser-based shortlist. Items stay on your device and each product opens on Amazon for purchase.

Are recommendations medical advice?

No. Our content is general information. Consult a qualified professional for persistent concerns.
