## Start with the concern

Blackheads and oily congestion may suit salicylic acid, while redness-prone skin may prefer a simpler niacinamide-focused formula.

## Introduce one active

Use one new acne treatment at a time so you can judge tolerance and avoid an unnecessarily complicated routine.

## Protect the barrier

Pair targeted treatment with a gentle cleanser, moisturizer and daily sunscreen.

## Know when to seek help

Painful, persistent or scarring acne deserves advice from a qualified dermatologist.
