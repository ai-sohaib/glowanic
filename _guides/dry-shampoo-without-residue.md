## Apply before roots are very oily

A light preventive application can absorb oil more evenly than a heavy rescue application.

## Use distance and sections

Hold the product at the manufacturer-recommended distance and apply only to small root sections.

## Let it sit

Give the powder time to absorb before massaging or brushing through.

## Avoid buildup

Dry shampoo is not a replacement for cleansing. Wash the scalp regularly and stop if irritation develops.
