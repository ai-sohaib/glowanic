## Start lower

Use the lowest effective setting, especially for fine, fragile, bleached or damaged hair.

## Work with dry hair

Unless a tool is specifically designed otherwise, do not use a flat iron on wet hair.

## Use heat protection

Distribute heat protectant evenly and allow hair to dry before styling.

## Limit repeat passes

Small, detangled sections and steady movement can reduce repeated high-heat passes.
