## Read manufacturer guidance

Electronic devices and specialty materials may require specific cleaning methods.

## Clean makeup brushes regularly

Wash bristles with a mild cleanser, keep the ferrule from soaking and dry brushes horizontally or bristles-down.

## Disinfect suitable hard tools

Use an appropriate method for stainless tools and never share tools that can break skin without proper sanitation.

## Dry fully before storage

Moisture trapped in closed containers can encourage odor and contamination.
