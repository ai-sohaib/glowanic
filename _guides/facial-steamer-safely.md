## Keep comfortable distance

Steam should feel warm and gentle, never painfully hot. Follow device instructions.

## Keep sessions brief

Longer is not automatically better. Start with a short session and avoid frequent overuse.

## Use clean water and equipment

Use the water type recommended by the manufacturer and clean the reservoir regularly.

## Follow with simple care

Pat dry and use a gentle moisturizer. Avoid strong actives immediately afterward if skin feels sensitive.
