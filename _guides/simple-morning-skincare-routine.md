## Step 1: Gentle cleansing

Use a mild cleanser or rinse based on your skin type. Avoid starting the day with a harsh, stripping wash.

## Step 2: A focused antioxidant

Vitamin C can support a brighter-looking tone. Begin with a comfortable formula and do not stack too many actives.

## Step 3: Barrier-supporting moisture

Choose hydration based on your climate and skin feel. Ceramides and humectants can support comfort.

## Step 4: Broad-spectrum sunscreen

Sunscreen is the final morning skincare step. Apply a generous, even layer and reapply as needed.
